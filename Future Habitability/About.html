The Project

What Exactly is our Goal?

For this project, our group wanted to show future climate changes, and employ a real life example of why people need to start paying attention to the environment around us. This map will hopefully shed light for those who are unsure where they want to settle down at, whatever that indecisive reason may be. Finally, before they end up getting comfortable in their new homes, users have the chance to rule out any locations that may end up underwater and on fire in the next 10-20 years.
